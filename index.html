Welcome

Cybersecurity is everyone’s responsibility. Learn about threats and how to stay safe online.

🎣 Phishing

Fraudulent attempts to steal sensitive information via fake emails or websites.

Defense: Verify senders, avoid suspicious links, enable spam filters.

💾 SQL Injection

Attackers exploit database query vulnerabilities to access or manipulate data.

Defense: Use parameterized queries, sanitize inputs, apply least-privilege DB access.

🦠 Malware Attacks

Viruses, worms, and ransomware designed to damage or steal data.

Defense: Keep software updated, use antivirus tools, avoid untrusted downloads.

🔑 Password Attacks

Hackers use brute-force or dictionary attacks to crack weak passwords.

Defense: Enforce strong password policies, enable MFA, use password managers.

🌐 Denial of Service (DDoS)

Flooding servers with traffic to make them unavailable.

Defense: Use firewalls, rate limiting, and DDoS protection services.

📡 Man-in-the-Middle (MITM)

Intercepting communication between two parties to steal or alter data.

Defense: Use HTTPS/TLS, VPNs, avoid public Wi-Fi for sensitive transactions.
